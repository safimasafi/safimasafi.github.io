Safeya Alblooshi is a student and practicing sound artist who engages with electronic music, sound art and experimental composition.
Originally from Russia and the UAE, and currently based in Abu Dhabi, Safeya is finishing her fourth year at the New York University Abu Dhabi as a Music major with a minor in Interactive Media, and is in the process of realising her senior capstone; Amplified Liquid Contaminants, a sound performance and installation.
